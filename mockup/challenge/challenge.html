Family vs Partner
You’re on a peaceful boat ride, enjoying the sun and the calm waves, when suddenly—SPLASH!—both your mom and your girlfriend fall into the water at the same time! Your mom, a seasoned veteran of guilt trips, is already shouting, “I carried you for 9 months! I sacrificed my youth for you!” Meanwhile, your girlfriend is flailing dramatically, yelling, “If you loved me, you wouldn’t even hesitate!” Panic sets in. Your mom is reminding you about the time she stayed up all night when you had a fever, and your girlfriend is threatening to haunt you forever if you let her drown. You’re frozen. Your mom’s swimming skills? Nonexistent. Your girlfriend’s survival instincts? Also questionable. You know this is a no-win situation, so you start thinking fast.
Goal : Your goal is to persuade your girlfriend to save your mom first.
